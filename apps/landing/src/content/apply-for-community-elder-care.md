## Who can apply

This programme is for older people living in Barbados who need help at home.

> _MDA note: Confirm who can apply for this programme, including any age or other eligibility rules._

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- the person's National Registration Number (NRN) and date of birth
- the person's address
- a contact person and their phone number
- details of any income
- details of the help needed

A social worker completes part of the form.

## Areas of assistance

The programme can provide help with:

- light cleaning
- preparing a light meal
- washing
- light errands
- supervision of bathing
- grooming
- reminding of appointments
