If your child is starting or continuing at a Barbadian primary school for the new school year, you can apply for a $100 grant towards the cost of their school uniform. You can claim one grant per child each school year, paid into your bank or credit union account.

## Who can apply

You can apply if your child is enrolled at a Barbadian primary school for the current or upcoming school year.

You can claim one grant per child per school year, and you can only apply once for each child in that year.

## How to apply

You can apply online. It takes about 10 minutes.

<a data-start-link href="/education/apply-for-a-school-uniform-grant/start">Start now</a>

## What the form asks for

You’ll need to give:

1. Your details as the parent or guardian — your name, National ID number, home address, parish, mobile number and email address.
2. Your child’s details — their full name, date of birth, school, school year and class level.
3. Your bank or credit union account details so the grant can be paid.

You’ll also need to confirm that your information is true and correct, and that you have not already applied for this grant for this child this school year.

## After you apply

The Ministry of Educational Transformation will check your child’s enrolment with their school. Once your application is approved, the $100 grant is paid into your account within 4 weeks. You’ll get a confirmation email and a reference number you can use to track your application.

If you have not heard anything within 4 weeks, you can call +1 (246) 535-0700 or email grants@mes.gov.bb.
