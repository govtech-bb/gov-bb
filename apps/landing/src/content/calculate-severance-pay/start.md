You should complete the calculator in one go. At the moment, it is not possible to save your answers and come back to them later.

This tool only gives an **estimate** based on the Severance Payments Act (Cap. 355A). Your contract of employment may entitle you to more. It is not legal advice.

## How long does it take?

About 3 minutes.

## What you will need

Have these ready before you start:

- why you were sent home (redundancy, disaster, lay-off or short time, or death of employer)
- your start date and your last day at work
- your usual gross pay (weekly or monthly) — include overtime or bonuses
