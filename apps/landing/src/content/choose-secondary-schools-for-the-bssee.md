## Who can use this service

You can use this service if you are the parent or guardian of a child sitting the BSSEE. Use it to rank the secondary schools you would like your child to attend. 

## How to choose your schools

You can complete the form online. It takes about 15 minutes.

<a data-start-link href="/education/choose-secondary-schools-for-the-bssee/start">Start now</a>

## What the form asks for

- your child’s name, current address, date of birth, sex, National ID number, current primary school and country of birth
- your details as the parent or guardian, including your title, name, National ID number, phone number and email address.
- your child’s assigned zone
- 9 school choices: 2 from any school and 7 from your assigned zone
- whether you are willing to consider a private school placement.
- a declaration confirming that the information provided is accurate, that you are submitting only one form, and that you understand how placement works

## After you submit your choices

Once your form has been processed, you will receive a reference number and a confirmation email will be sent to the email address you provided.

You will be told your child’s school placement within 4 to 6 weeks of the results being released.

If you need help, contact the Ministry of Educational Transformation at 535-0600.
