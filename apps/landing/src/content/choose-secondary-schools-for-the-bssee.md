If your child is sitting the Barbados Secondary School Entrance Examination (BSSEE), use this form to rank the secondary schools you would like them to attend.

## Who can apply

You can apply if you are the parent or guardian of a child sitting the BSSEE.

## How to apply

You can complete the online form. It takes about 15 minutes.

<a data-start-link href="/education/choose-secondary-schools-for-the-bssee/start">Start now</a>

## What the form asks for

1. Your child’s current primary school, name, date of birth, sex, National ID number and country of birth.
2. Your child’s address and email address.
3. Your details as the parent or guardian, including your title, name, National ID number, phone number and email address.
4. Your child’s assigned zone.
5. Nine ranked school choices: 2 from any school and 7 from your assigned zone.
6. Whether you are willing to consider a private school placement.

You also confirm that the information is accurate, that you are submitting only one form, and that you understand how placement works.

## After you apply

You will get a reference number, and a confirmation email is sent to the address you provide once your form is processed.

You will be told your child’s school assignment within 4 to 6 weeks of the results being released.
