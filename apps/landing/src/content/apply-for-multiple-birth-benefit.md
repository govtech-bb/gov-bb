Use this service to apply for the Multiple Birth Benefit from the Social Empowerment Agency (SEA), for a parent or guardian of twins or triplets.

> _MDA note: Confirm the correct category/breadcrumb on alpha.gov.bb._

## Who can apply

You can apply as the mother, father, or guardian of children from the same birth. This means twins or triplets.

> _MDA note: Confirm any further eligibility for this benefit, such as residency, income, or the age of the children._

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- your ID and contact details
- your address
- details of each child, including their full name, ID number, and school or nursery
- your bank or credit union account details

## Documents you must provide

You must provide these documents. Your application will not be processed without them.

- a copy of your National Registration Card
- a birth certificate for each child
- a National Registration Card for each child
- your banking details

All copies must be certified.
