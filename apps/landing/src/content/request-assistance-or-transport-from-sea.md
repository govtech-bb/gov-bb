Use this service to request assistance, or transport for a person with a disability, from the Social Empowerment Agency (SEA).

> _MDA note: Confirm the correct category/breadcrumb on alpha.gov.bb._

## Who can apply

This service is for people living in Barbados who need assistance or transport from the Social Empowerment Agency.

> _MDA note: Confirm who can apply (the form does not state eligibility). Confirm whether someone can apply on behalf of another person._

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- your ID and contact details
- your address
- a next of kin and their contact details
- details of the assistance or transport you need
- details of any disability or health condition
- how many people live in your household

If you are asking for transport, you will also give the appointment date and where you need to go.
