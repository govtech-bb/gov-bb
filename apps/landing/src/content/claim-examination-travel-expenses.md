If you are a teacher or a member of exam-centre staff, you can claim reimbursement for travel you made on official examination business. This includes travel for invigilation, marking sessions, exam centre setup, and collecting or returning papers.

## Who can claim

This service is for teachers and exam-centre staff claiming reimbursement for travel made on official examination business.

## Before you start

There is no fee to make a claim. You submit one claim per month, and each form covers a single travel period of one month.

Mileage claims do not need receipts but must follow the published Civil Service mileage rate. For other expenses, you’ll need to upload your receipts.

The form takes about 10 to 15 minutes, depending on how many trips you are claiming for.

## How to claim

<a data-start-link href="/education/claim-examination-travel-expenses/start">Start now</a>

## What the form asks for

1. Your personal details: surname, first names, National ID number, your school or exam centre, home address, mobile number and email address.
2. The travel period: the month and year you are claiming for.
3. The number of trips you are claiming, and for each trip: the date, where you travelled from and to, the purpose, and the amount claimed in Barbados dollars.
4. Supporting receipts, where they are needed for non-mileage expenses.
5. A declaration confirming the trips were on official examination business, that the amounts are accurate, and that you understand the penalty for false information.

## After you claim

The Exams Officer reviews and approves your claim, and the Deputy Permanent Secretary and Account Officer authorise payment. Reimbursement is paid through your school payroll, and you can expect it within 6 weeks. A confirmation email is sent to the address you gave.

If you need help, you can contact the Ministry by phone on +1 (246) 535-1284 or by email at mist.forms@barbados.gov.bb.
