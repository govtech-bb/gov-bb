Volunteer with a Centre Management Committee to help review programme proposals and run your local community centre.

## Overview

Volunteer to review requests for use of community centres, review programme proposals, and facilitate extended centre access. Must show desire for positive socio-economic change, deep understanding of the community, and credible knowledge of relevant skill sets.

## Who can apply

- Interest in volunteer, community, leadership

## How to apply

Complete the short application form to register your interest. Your progress is saved automatically.
