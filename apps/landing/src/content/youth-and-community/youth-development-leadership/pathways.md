A job-attachment and mentorship programme for unemployed young people aged 16 to 24, with support from social workers and counsellors.

## Overview

Job attachment and mentorship programme. Life and job skills, work ethic, employment opportunities. Includes psycho-social support from social workers and counsellors. Targets unemployed youth not in full-time education.

## Who can apply

- Ages 16–24
- Interest in employment, mentorship, career

## How to apply

Complete the short application form to register your interest. Your progress is saved automatically.
