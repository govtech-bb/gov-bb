Free vocational training in over 20 trades — from motor vehicle engineering and cookery to construction and healthcare — through Project Dawn.

## Overview

Community development unit under the Division of Youth and Culture. Focused on transforming neighbourhood blocks through youth-led engagement.

## Who can apply

- Interest in community, development

## Sub-programmes

### Project Dawn

Free vocational training in 20+ courses: Motor Vehicle Engineering, Professional Housekeeping, Basic Trade Cookery, Bar Operations, hospitality, beauty, construction, healthcare.

## How to apply

Complete the short application form to register your interest. Your progress is saved automatically.

[Apply now](https://alpha.gov.bb/youth-and-community/skills-trades-vocational-training/btu/form)
