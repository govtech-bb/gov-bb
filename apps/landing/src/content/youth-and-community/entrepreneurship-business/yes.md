One-to-one support and access to funding to help young people turn a business idea into a working enterprise.

## Overview

From idea to enterprise. Direct technical assistance from Youth Enterprise Officers and consultants, plus access to financial assistance via partnerships.

## Who can apply

- Interest in business, entrepreneurship

## How to apply

Complete the short application form to register your interest. Your progress is saved automatically.
