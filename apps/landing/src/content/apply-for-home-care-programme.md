## Who can apply

This service is for older people in Barbados who need help at home.

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- the person's National Registration Number (NRN) and date of birth
- the person's address
- a contact person and their phone number
- details of any pensions or other income
- details of the person's health, mobility, and the help they need

Part of the assessment is completed by a social worker.
