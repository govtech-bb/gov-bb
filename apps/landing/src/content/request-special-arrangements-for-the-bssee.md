If your child needs special arrangements to sit the Barbados Secondary Schools’ Entrance Examination (BSSEE) on equal terms, you can request access arrangements. Examples include extra time, a separate room, large-print papers, a reader or a medical adjustment.

There is no fee.

## Who can apply

You can apply if you are the parent or guardian of a child taking the BSSEE.

## Before you start

Submit your request at least 6 weeks before the BSSEE so the Ministry has time to set up the arrangement.

The decision is at the Ministry’s discretion.

## How to apply

You can complete the online form. It takes about 10 minutes.

## What the form asks for

1. Your details as the parent or guardian, including your title, name, National ID number, home address, mobile number and email address.
2. Your child’s name and date of birth.
3. Which BSSEE sitting year you are applying for.
4. Your child’s current school and class.
5. The type of arrangement needed, such as medical, learning, physical or other.
6. A description of the arrangement needed and the supporting reason.

Depending on the type of request, you may need to upload a medical, specialist or psychologist report. You can also upload the Principal’s letter and any other supporting document.

You confirm that the information is true and correct, that your child’s Principal is aware of the request, that the Ministry may share information with exam centre staff, and that the decision is at the Ministry’s discretion.

## After you apply

You will get a reference number, and a copy is sent to your email address.

The Ministry will review your request, consult your child’s primary school Principal, and respond within 3 weeks by letter and phone. If approved, the arrangement is communicated to the exam centre before exam day.
