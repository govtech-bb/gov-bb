You can request an official statement of your past examination results from the Ministry of Educational Transformation. This covers exams such as CSEC, CAPE, BSSEE, City and Guilds, Cambridge and GCE.

## Who can apply

This service is for anyone who needs an official statement of results for exams they sat through the Ministry. You can also apply on behalf of someone else if you have their authorisation.

## Before you start

You must pay the processing fee before you submit the form. Pay at the Ministry’s Accounts Department, then upload the receipt as proof. Statements are not issued until the fee is paid and the receipt is verified.

You’ll need to upload:

1. A government-issued photo ID.
2. Your receipt of payment.
3. An authorisation letter, if you are applying on behalf of someone else.

The form takes about 10 minutes to complete.

## What the form asks for

1. Your title, surname as it appeared on your exam entry, first names and any maiden or former name.
2. Your National ID number, date of birth, home address, mobile number and email address.
3. The examination body, the series or sitting, your candidate number if you have it, and the school or centre where you sat the exam.
4. The subjects you want on the statement and your reason for needing it.
5. How you’d like to receive the statement: collect in person at the Examinations Unit, by post, or by courier. If you choose post or courier, you’ll give a delivery address.

## After you apply

The Examinations Unit verifies your identity and payment, locates your records and prepares your statement. Statements are normally ready within 10 working days and sent by the method you chose. A confirmation email is sent to the address you gave.

If you need help, you can contact the Examinations Unit by phone on (246) 535-0600 or by email at exams@mes.gov.bb.
