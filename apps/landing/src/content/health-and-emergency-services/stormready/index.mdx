<Notice>
  **Hurricane season** runs from **1 June to 30 November 2026**. Prepare before
  the season starts — do not wait for a storm warning.
</Notice>

<Section heading="Household checklist" id="checklist-heading">

Use this checklist to make sure your household is ready for a hurricane. It covers water, food, documents, first aid, communication, and the steps to take before a storm.

### Highlights from the checklist

<Highlights>
  <Highlight title="Water">
    Store at least 3 gallons of drinking water per person for 3 days. Fill
    baths, buckets and other containers before a storm.
  </Highlight>
  <Highlight title="Documents">
    Keep your national ID, insurance documents and birth certificates in a
    waterproof bag. Have cash in small bills available.
  </Highlight>
  <Highlight title="Communication">
    Charge all devices and power banks. Keep a battery-powered radio to receive
    DEM updates. Write down important phone numbers and emergency contacts.
  </Highlight>
  <Highlight title="Before a storm">
    Board up windows if needed, bring in outdoor items, fill your vehicle with
    gas, and make sure family members know your emergency plan and shelter
    arrangements.
  </Highlight>
</Highlights>
