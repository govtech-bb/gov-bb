Applications close on **[date — to be confirmed]**.

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- your Barbados National Identification (ID) card, as you will need to enter the
  number and upload a digital copy
- contact details for your emergency contact
- details of any medicine or allergies
- dates and details of your camp, work or volunteer experience
- dates and details of your education and training
- contact details for 3 people who can give a reference and are not relatives
- digital copies of 2 reference letters
- a digital copy of your health certificate

## Cost

There is no cost to apply.

## How long it takes

The application takes about 20 to 30 minutes to complete.

It may take longer if you need to find or upload your documents.

## What happens after you apply

The Division of Youth Affairs, Division of Youth and Culture, Prime Minister's
Office will review your application.

They may contact you if they need more information.

## If you need help

Contact the Division of Youth Affairs, Division of Youth and Culture, Prime
