The National Housing Corporation (NHC) rents homes and lots to people on lower incomes. Use this service to apply for an NHC rental unit or lot for you and your family.

## Who can apply

You can apply if your household income is **$3,000 a month or less**.

<!-- MDA NOTE: The $3,000 monthly household income limit is taken from the form but not yet confirmed. It is the main eligibility gate — confirm it is current, and whether it is gross or net, before publishing. -->

You can apply on your own, or with a co-applicant, such as a partner or family member who will live in the unit with you.

<!-- MDA NOTE: Confirm whether non-Barbadians can apply, whether there is an age limit, and whether existing NHC tenants can apply for a different unit. -->

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- your National Registration Number (NRN)
- your TAMIS number
- contact details we can reach you on
- details of your employer, salary, and pay period
- details of everyone who will live with you: their name, age, relation to you, and whether they work or are in school

You will also need to upload:

- two recent pay slips
- a job letter
- a photo ID
- a passport-size photo of yourself

If you are applying with a co-applicant, they will need the same information and documents.
