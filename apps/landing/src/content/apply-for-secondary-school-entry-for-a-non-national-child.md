Use this service to apply for a non-national child — or a child born outside Barbados — to attend a government secondary school.

There is no application fee.

## Who can apply

You can apply for a child who is a non-national or who was born outside Barbados.

If the child has refugee, asylum or stateless status, these cases need a different process — contact the Ministry directly.

## Before you start

Gather your documents first. You will need:

1. The child’s birth certificate.
2. If you are a CARICOM national, your immigration status certificate.
3. If you are not a CARICOM national, the child’s immigration status certificate.

You can upload files in PDF, JPG or PNG format, up to 10 MB each. You can also choose to submit physical copies in person.

## How to apply

It takes about 10 minutes to apply online.

## What the form asks for

1. The child’s first name, surname and any other names.
2. The child’s country of birth, sex and date of birth.
3. The name of the school the child currently attends.
4. The preferred secondary school in Barbados.
5. Your title, name, address in Barbados, contact number, email address and country of birth.
6. Your immigration status in Barbados.
7. Documents to upload.
8. A declaration confirming the information you have given is true and correct.

## After you apply

You will get a reference number when you submit your application.

1. The Ministry will review your application and the documents.
2. You will be contacted by the school within 4 weeks.
3. A confirmation email will be sent to the address you gave.

If you chose to submit physical copies of your documents, take them to the school as soon as possible to avoid delays.
