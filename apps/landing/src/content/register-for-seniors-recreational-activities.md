Use this service to register for the Seniors' Recreational Activities Programme. The programme runs weekly recreational sessions for older people at community locations around Barbados.

## Who can register

This programme is for older people living in Barbados.

> _MDA note: Confirm the age or other eligibility for this programme (the form asks for age but states no limit)._

## What you need before you start

You will need:

- your Barbados National Registration Number (NRN)
- a contact number
- your address, including your parish
- an emergency contact and their phone number
- details of any health conditions or disability, if you have any

When you register, you choose the location and day that suit you.
